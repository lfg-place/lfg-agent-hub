About This Workshop

The Year Doesn't Build Itself

Most agents close out the year, take a breath, and then stumble into the next one doing the same things. No clear targets. No written plan. No way to know if they're on track until it's too late to course-correct.

This workshop is built for agents doing 0–19 deals a year who are ready to change that. We're going to walk through the exact goal-setting process that top producers use — not the fluffy vision-board version, but the specific, trackable, reverse-engineered kind that tells you exactly what you need to do every week to hit your number.

You'll leave with a written business plan for 2027, a daily and weekly activity target, and the clarity to actually execute it.

What You'll Learn

What We're Covering

🎯
Setting Goals That Actually Stick

The difference between a wish and a goal is a plan. We'll show you how to set targets that are specific, measurable, and tied to real activity.

🔢
Reverse-Engineering Your Number

Start with your income goal and work backwards — how many closings, how many appointments, how many conversations do you need every single week?

📅
Building Your 2027 Business Plan

You'll build it in the room, not just think about it. Leave with a written plan that has your targets, your lead sources, and your weekly activity numbers.

⏱️
Time Blocking for Execution

A plan without a schedule is just hope. We'll time-block your week so your goals have protected time on the calendar before anything else.

📊
Tracking What Matters

The numbers that tell you if you're on track — and how to review them weekly so you can adjust before small gaps become big problems.

🧠
Mindset & Commitment

Most plans fail because of psychology, not strategy. We'll cover how to stay accountable to your goals when motivation fades — which it always does.

Who Should Attend

This Is for You If...

✓
You're closing fewer than 20 deals a year and you're ready to change that — not someday, but next year.
✓
You've never had a written business plan — or had one and never looked at it after January.
✓
You want to know exactly what to do each week to hit your number, instead of guessing.
✓
You want to walk out of a room with a real plan in your hands — not just ideas in your head.
