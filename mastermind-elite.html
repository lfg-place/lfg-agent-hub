About This Mastermind

A Room for Operators, Not Spectators

The Elite Agent Mastermind is a closed-door session built exclusively for agents already closing 25 or more transactions a year. This isn't a workshop for agents trying to get started — it's a room for operators comparing notes on what's actually working at a high level: scaling a team, protecting margin, leverage, and the next-level systems that separate a good year from a great one.

Because the conversation only works with the right people in the room, attendance is by invitation only. There is no public registration for this event. If you qualify and haven't heard from us, reach out directly to Cory or Drew.

Special Guest

Featuring Brian Gubernick

Guest Speaker

Brian Gubernick

20+ Year Real Estate Veteran · Team Leader · Coach · Podcast Host

Brian Gubernick has worn just about every hat in this business — agent, team leader, brokerage partner, investor, and entrepreneur. He built Homehelper Consultants into one of the top 250 producing teams in the country (as ranked by RealTrends/Wall Street Journal), expanded it across multiple markets, and has personally coached thousands of sales professionals on business growth, leadership, and performance.

These days, Brian channels that experience into No Days Off — a daily podcast built around the idea that every day is an opportunity to get better in business, relationships, and life. He also co-founded Metrix Masterminds, where he's spent nearly a decade hosting world-class speakers and facilitating high-level conversations among top producers.

At this mastermind, Brian will be diving into Benjamin Hardy's The Science of Scaling — breaking down what those frameworks actually mean for how serious agents and team leaders build, grow, and operate at a higher level.

Brian brings a rare combination: serious production credentials and a genuine passion for elevating the people around him. We're excited to have him in the room.
